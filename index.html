Our Story

Toronto Roots.
Future-Friendly Finishes.

For more than twenty years, Eco Painting has helped homeowners and businesses refresh their spaces with durable coatings and a lighter environmental footprint. We grew through referrals and repeat clients — and we still measure success one happy customer at a time.

We still believe in the most reliable source of marketing: word of mouth. With community as the foundation of our business, we take pride in what we do and stop at nothing until all of our clients are fully satisfied — even the ones with the most stringent requirements.

Quality work takes time and skilled labour. All of our painters are experienced, knowledgeable, and friendly professionals. Speed is not a requirement and it is not rewarded.
